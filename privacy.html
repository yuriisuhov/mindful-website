Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Mindful. is a meditation timer app for Apple Watch. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how it's used, and your rights.

Data We Access

Apple Health (HealthKit)

Mindful. requests access to Apple Health to save your meditation sessions as Mindful Minutes. This data is written to Apple Health on your device. We do not read, collect, or retrieve any health data from Apple Health.

Session Data

Your meditation session history (duration, date, and streaks) is stored locally on your Apple Watch using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No data is sent to external servers

Third-Party Services

Mindful. does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Apple Watch. We do not operate servers or databases that store user data. If you delete the app, all locally stored session data is removed.

Children's Privacy

Mindful. does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@mindfultimer.app.
